While working in local government, I became fascinated with the potential of the new Smart Cities movement. Digital platforms have created a new commons and have the ability to reach and engage with people in a wholly new manner. And, on the other side, new developments in the accesibility of data give us new evidence on which to base policies. I quickly recognized that in order to properly utilize these new tools, I would have to learn as much about them as I could.

After spending a number of months learning web development my own, I dedicated myself to a 4-month, full-stack bootcamp at DigitalCrafts in Atlanta, Georgia. I found the puzzles of coding fascinating and consuming, often ruminating on unsolved problems well after I was supposed to have been done coding for the day. While at DigitalCrafts, I honed my skills and focused on a full-stack of JavaScript: Express.js, React, Node.js, and more. I look forward to spending my days addressing those hard problems that keep me thinking for hours, and I want to use these tools to keep making a better future.
